Story

Xiao Ran was born in 2013 in Guizhou Province with a complex congenital heart disease involving multiple defects: a Ventricular Septal Defect (VSD), an Atrial Septal Defect (ASD), and a Patent Ductus Arteriosus (PDA). This combination of conditions placed severe strain on his heart and significantly limited his ability to engage in normal childhood activities.

Throughout his early years, Xiao Ran struggled with chronic fatigue, shortness of breath, and frequent respiratory infections. Simple activities that other children took for granted—running, playing sports, or even walking up stairs—were exhausting challenges for him. His condition also affected his growth and development, causing concern for his family and doctors.

Xiao Ran's family, living in a rural area of Guizhou Province, faced significant financial barriers to accessing the specialized cardiac care he needed. The complexity of his condition required surgery at a well-equipped hospital with experienced cardiac surgeons, which was far beyond their financial means.

In 2025, Heart to Heart raised ¥30,000 to fund Xiao Ran's complex heart surgery. The operation, performed in January 2025, successfully repaired all three defects. The surgical team reported excellent outcomes, and Xiao Ran's recovery has exceeded expectations.

Today, Xiao Ran is experiencing life with a properly functioning heart for the first time. His energy levels have dramatically improved, and he can now participate in school activities and play with his friends without the constant burden of breathlessness and fatigue. His teachers note that he is more engaged in class and his overall quality of life has been transformed.

Impact

Xiao Ran's case represents one of the most complex and impactful interventions supported by Heart to Heart. His successful surgery demonstrates the life-changing difference that community support can make for children facing severe congenital heart conditions. With his heart now functioning normally, Xiao Ran has the opportunity to grow, learn, and thrive like any other child his age.

His family's gratitude is immeasurable, and they express their thanks to every donor and supporter who made this miracle possible. Ran's story serves as a powerful reminder of why Heart to Heart's mission is so vital.
